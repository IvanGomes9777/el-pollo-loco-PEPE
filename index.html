Pepe Peligroso

This is Pepe, he is known for having a big farm with chickens und other animals. One morning , not very different from the others, Pepe woke up to hear all the chickens screaming like cazy. He stepped outside and saw that all the chicken tried to attack him. One chicken even grew ten times bigger than the rest. But everyone knows Pepe, he is not afraid of anything. He decided to save his farm from the crazy chicken. The small chicken Pepe eliminated very quickly, but for the big chicken Pepe grabbed some bottles of El Yuceteco a mexican sauce as hot as the sun. Finally Pepe won against that chicken as well and celebrated while sitting on his porch and drink a handfull of corona bears.
